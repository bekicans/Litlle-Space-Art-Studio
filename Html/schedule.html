Little Makers

This class is for 3-5 yr olds.

Little Space Art Studio create a nurturing and happy environment where children learn to express themselves through the arts with confidence and joy.

Toddler Art Playgroup

Toddler Art Play Group is a forty five minute free form art class where walking toddlers to 3 years can investigate and learn through inspired art and sensory experiences.

Toddler Art Play Group has been described as the best messy play date you’ll ever have, at someone else’s house. Enjoy. Clean up is on us.

Open Studio Day

Come with your child to open studio time. Kids are able to work with engaging materials, including clay, paints, wire, beads, collage, etc. to create, tinker and experiment with their own ideas.

This is a fantastic opportunity for children to explore their own creativity at each individual pace while parents and staff help them in bringing their ideas to fruition. Invitations to create will be set up as well.

Large-scale Mural Workshop

Our Large-scale mural class is for kids ages 6 & UP. Each student will create a mural week after week using different mediums, tools and techniques to create their very own masterpiece.

Drawing and Painting

Join us in the studio for a Drawing and Painting class for 4-6 year olds. Students will learn the basics of drawing through multiple fun drawing exercises.
