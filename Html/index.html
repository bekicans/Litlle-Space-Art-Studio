Little Space Art Studio provides high quality art instruction taught by experienced artists and educators. We value collaboration, creativity, and community in order to provide a supportive and inspiring learning environment. We offer classes and workshops in a variety mediums and techniques.

Little Space Art Studio is a process based art studio in Novi Sad. Process Art is art that’s all about the making and the doing, rather than the finished product. If you live in Novi Sad and have a child that loves art, or a child that you’d love to expose to more sensory and art experiences, come join us! Little Space hosts art classes, birthday parties, toddler play groups, mobile art parties and classes, ladies craft nights and workshops at our studio.

Please email littlespace@gmail.com with any inquiries.

Inspiring space

Children are inspired and influenced by their environment. At The Art Studio, you will find a calm and aesthetically pleasing space full of high quality materials and professional art supplies. We provide a place for self-expression in our creative and supportive studio.
